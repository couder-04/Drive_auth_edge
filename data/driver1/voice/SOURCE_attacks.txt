attack_other_speaker/: macOS `say` TTS stand-ins (Aman/Daniel/Fred/Flo/Junior)
  same payment phrases as enroll. Replace with a real 2nd human when available.

To re-do other_speaker with a friend (preferred):
  MIC=":default"
  for i in 01 02 03 04 05; do
    echo "Other person speaks now ($i)"
    ffmpeg -y -f avfoundation -i "$MIC" -ar 16000 -ac 1 -t 3 \
      data/driver1/voice/attack_other_speaker/other_$i.wav
    sleep 2
  done
